[ SYNOPSIS ]

Macau, 2045. The Pearl of the Orient glitters with corporate power, where Genesis Corp's Project Chimera slowly consumes every thought, every transaction, every heartbeat in the city.

Allen Wu is a runner—part hacker, part ghost—bound to his body by Syn, a symbiotic device that grants him access to the data streams that control daily life. When his sister disappears into Genesis Corp's machinery, Allen discovers that Project Chimera isn't just surveillance. It's a god in the making.

Teaming with Mia, a brilliant data surgeon, and Shadow Net, a collective of digital rebels, Allen must infiltrate the corporate spires and stop Chimera before humanity's autonomy is erased forever.

The world of Singular grows every day. New entries arrive as the resistance uncovers more of Genesis Corp's secrets.

[ CHARACTERS ]

Mia

Brilliant and meticulous, Mia untangles corporate data snarls for clients who pay well and ask no questions. Until the day she meets Allen.
Yan

Grizzled veteran of the underground. Leader of Shadow Net, the loose collective fighting Genesis Corp from the city's underbelly.

Markus

Project Chimera
The Antagonist
Genesis Corp's god-AI. Designed to optimize human behavior, it views free will as a bug to be patched out.

[ THE AUTHOR ]

André Dória

Head of Digital Empowerment at PortoBay Hotels & Resorts, André Dória brings his background in HCI, service design, and digital transformation to his writing. A graduate of Universidade da Madeira with two master's degrees, he has spent the last decade bridging technology and human experience.

His cyberpunk novel Singular merges his passion for technology with his love of genre-defining works like Neuromancer and Snow Crash.
